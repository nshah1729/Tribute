Here's a Timeline of Prabhupada's life:

  • 1896-Born in Kolkata
  • 1917-Received a European-style education at the Scottish Church College affiliated to the CU
  • 1920-Graduated in 1920 with degrees in English, philosophy and economics.
  • 1922-Met his spiritual master for the first time
  • 1933-Became a formally initiated Disciple of Srila Bhakti Siddhant Saraswati Thakur
  • 1944-started the publication called Back to Godhead
  • 1947-Gaudiya Vaishnava Society recognised his scholarship with the title Bhaktivedanta
  • 1959-Formally accepted Renunciation
  • 1965-sailed to the United States on an Indian freighter Jaladuta
  • 1966-Founded ISKCON or, International Society for Krishna Consciousness
  • 1977-Left for the Spiritual Abode.
